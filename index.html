The problem

Expert knowledge shouldn't be a single point of failure

A GPU fleet requires deep expertise — Kubernetes, NVIDIA stack, cost models, incident correlation across a dozen tools. When your senior SRE is unavailable, the team slows down. When incidents fire at 2 AM, every minute of diagnostic costs real money.

🔧

Expertise bottleneck

kubectl, Helm, DCGM, MIG, ArgoCD — mastering the stack takes months. Operations depend on the people who have done it before, not on documented, repeatable workflows.

⏱

Incidents are slow to diagnose

A GPU saturation alert fires. Correlating logs, metrics, events, and deployment state across 4 tools takes 20 minutes before the first action. Every minute costs money.

💸

GPU waste is invisible until the bill arrives

Idle GPUs, over-provisioned replicas, orphaned workloads — at $2–8/GPU/hour, waste compounds silently. Without a FinOps layer, you're flying blind on actual ROI.

The solution

The intelligence layer on top of your GPU stack

VibOps encodes GPU/AI infrastructure expertise into a conversational operations platform. Your team works in natural language — VibOps translates every intent into the right kubectl, Helm, or API call, with guardrails that protect production from mistakes.

🤖

Conversational operations

Deploy models, scale clusters, diagnose incidents, promote pipelines — from a conversation. Your kubectl runs. Your Helm deploys. The agent handles the orchestration.

🛡

Production guardrails

Destructive actions require explicit confirmation. Production namespaces are protected by team scopes. Guardrails are enforced at the engine level, not the UI level.

💡

FinOps built in

Idle GPU detection, budget alerts, per-team chargeback reports — the platform surfaces waste before it compounds and gives finance teams the data they need.

🧠

Memory across sessions

The agent remembers your infrastructure context. Every morning, a brief surfaces anomalies, cost trends, and pending actions — without you having to ask.

🔒

Sovereign by design

Deployed inside your infrastructure. Cluster state, credentials, and audit logs never leave your perimeter. On-prem LLM supported for fully air-gapped deployments.

⚙️

Automation pipelines

Define multi-step promotion workflows with rollback guards. Staging → health check → production → verify — triggered from a conversation, audited at every step.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

VibOps deploys as a lightweight control plane next to your existing infrastructure. No agents required on GPU nodes. No data exfiltration.

01

Deploy VibOps

Self-hosted via Helm or Docker Compose. Deploys inside your infrastructure — on-prem, cloud, or hybrid. Your perimeter, your control.

02

Connect your clusters

Install a lightweight Connect Gateway on each cluster. Auto-discovers namespaces, deployments, and GPU resources across any vendor stack.

03

Operate in natural language

Use the VibOps console directly, or connect Claude Desktop or Cursor via pip install vibops-mcp. Your AI assistant speaks natively to your stack.

04

Full audit trail

Every operation logged with user, timestamp, and exact command executed. Immutable. Compliance-ready from day one.
